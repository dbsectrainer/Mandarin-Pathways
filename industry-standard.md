## Overview

Mandarin Mastery is the next-generation language learning app designed from the ground up to address the unique challenges of learning Mandarin: **tones**, **characters**, **pronunciation**, and **cultural context**.

Inspired by the strengths of top apps like HelloChinese, Pleco, Rocket Chinese, and Anki — and addressing their limitations — Mandarin Mastery delivers a **seamless, comprehensive experience** that takes learners from absolute beginner (HSK 1) to advanced conversational fluency (HSK 6+).

No more switching between 5+ apps. Everything you need is here, intelligently integrated with AI personalization and real speaking practice.

## Key Features

### 🎯 **Beginner-Friendly Foundations**

- **Gamified Lessons** like HelloChinese: Bite-sized, engaging daily missions with visual tone diagrams, instant pronunciation feedback via advanced speech recognition.
- Progressive HSK-aligned curriculum covering pinyin, vocabulary, grammar, and sentences.
- Beautiful stroke-order animations and handwriting practice (inspired by Skritter).

### 📖 **Intelligent Dictionary & Reference**

- **Pleco-level powerhouse** built-in: Offline dictionary with 100,000+ entries, example sentences, audio, and cultural notes.
- **Live OCR Camera Translation**: Point your camera at signs, menus, or textbooks for instant translations.
- Flashcard mode with spaced repetition (SRS) powered by Anki-like algorithms.

### 🗣️ **Speaking & Listening Mastery**

- **AI Conversation Partner**: Practice dialogues with voice AI that corrects tones in real-time and simulates native speakers.
- Graded reading/listening materials (inspired by Du Chinese) with adjustable difficulty.
- Pimsleur-style audio lessons for on-the-go immersion.

### 🔄 **Smart Personalization & Retention**

- **Adaptive Learning Path**: AI analyzes your progress, weaknesses (e.g., specific tones or characters), and adjusts lessons automatically.
- Built-in Anki-style SRS across all content for long-term retention.
- Daily streaks, achievements, and leaderboards to maintain consistency.

### 🌍 **Immersion & Real-World Tools**

- Integration with Chinese media: Podcasts, short videos, news with interactive subtitles.
- Language exchange matching with native speakers.
- Cultural insights and situational modules (ordering food, business meetings, travel).

### 📊 **Progress Tracking**

- Detailed analytics dashboard.
- HSK mock tests and certification prep.
- Exportable progress reports.

# Mandarin Mastery - Product Roadmap

**Vision**: Become the definitive, all-in-one industry standard for learning Mandarin Chinese by integrating the best aspects of existing tools (HelloChinese, Pleco, Rocket Chinese, Anki, etc.) while leveraging modern AI, superior UX, and real-world practice.

**Core Principles**:

- Prioritize **tones**, **characters**, and **speaking confidence**.
- Deliver **delightful gamification** without sacrificing depth.
- Ensure **seamless offline + online** experience.
- Focus on measurable progress toward HSK levels and real conversational fluency.
- Iterate based on user feedback and retention metrics.

## Release Phases

### Phase 0: MVP (Minimum Viable Product) - v0.8 (Current)

**Goal**: Launch a polished core experience that outperforms single-purpose apps for beginners.

**Core Features**:

- Gamified beginner lessons (HSK 1-3) with tone visualization and speech recognition.
- Built-in advanced dictionary with OCR camera translation.
- Basic handwriting/stroke-order practice.
- Anki-style spaced repetition flashcards.
- Daily streaks, simple analytics, and progress tracking.
- Cross-platform (iOS, Android, Web) with offline support.

**Technical Foundations**:

- React Native + Next.js frontend.
- Node.js/Python backend with initial AI services.
- SQLite offline + cloud sync.
- Basic user authentication (email, Google, WeChat).

**Success Metrics**:

- 10,000 active users in first 3 months.
- 70%+ day-7 retention.
- Positive ratings vs. HelloChinese/Duolingo in user surveys.

**Timeline**: Q3 2026 (Target launch: August 2026)

### Phase 1: v1.0 - Official Launch (Polished All-in-One)

**Goal**: Deliver the complete "no app-switching" experience described in the README.

**Key Additions**:

- Full HSK 1-4 curriculum with grammar modules.
- AI Conversation Partner (real-time tone correction + dialogue simulation).
- Graded reading & listening library (Du Chinese style).
- Pimsleur-style audio-only lessons.
- Premium subscription (unlimited AI practice, ad-free, advanced stats).
- Placement test + adaptive learning paths.

- Cultural context modules.

**Polish & Infrastructure**:

- Performance optimization and full offline mode.
- Analytics dashboard (user progress, weak areas).

- Basic notifications and reminders.

**Timeline**: Q4 2026

**Target**: Support learners moving beyond beginner level.

**New Features**:
