
Restaurant Dialogue 1
---------------------
Waiter: Hello, how many people?
Customer: Two people, thank you.
Waiter: Please follow me.
Customer: Do you have a menu?
Waiter: Here you are, please take your time.

Restaurant Dialogue 2
---------------------
Customer: I'd like to order.
Waiter: What would you like to order?
Customer: I want one Kung Pao Chicken and a bowl of rice.
Waiter: OK, anything else?
Customer: Also a cup of tea, thank you.
