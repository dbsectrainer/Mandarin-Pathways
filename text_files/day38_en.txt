
Shopping Dialogue 1
-------------------
Customer: How much is this piece of clothing?
Clerk: 200 yuan.
Customer: That's too expensive. Can you make it cheaper?
Clerk: 180 yuan, can't go any lower.
Customer: OK, I'll take it.

Shopping Dialogue 2
-------------------
Customer: Excuse me, where is the fitting room?
Clerk: Over there, turn right.
Customer: Do you have this in a larger size?
Clerk: Let me check. Yes, here's an XL.
Customer: Thanks, I'll try it on.
