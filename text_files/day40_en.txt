
Travel Dialogue 1
-----------------
Tourist: Excuse me, how do I get to the Great Wall?
Local: You can take the subway to Beijing North Station, then transfer to bus route 916.
Tourist: Approximately how long will it take?
Local: About two hours.
Tourist: Thank you for your help!
