Beginner Translation Exercises
--------------------

Translate simple sentences between English and Chinese

1. Chinese: 我叫李明。
Translation: My name is Li Ming.

2. Chinese: 我是学生。
Translation: I am a student.

3. Chinese: 我喜欢吃中国菜。
Translation: I like to eat Chinese food.

4. Chinese: 洗手间在哪里？
Translation: Where is the bathroom?

5. Chinese: 这个多少钱？
Translation: How much does this cost?
