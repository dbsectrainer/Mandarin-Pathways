Intermediate Translation Exercises
--------------------

Translate more complex sentences between English and Chinese

1. Chinese: 我学习中文已经三年了。
Translation: I have been studying Chinese for three years.

2. Chinese: 虽然很难，但是我喜欢学习中文。
Translation: Although it's difficult, I enjoy learning Chinese.

3. Chinese: 你能说得慢一点吗？
Translation: Could you please speak more slowly?

4. Chinese: 我计划明年去中国旅行。
Translation: I plan to travel to China next year.

5. Chinese: 你最喜欢的中国菜是什么？
Translation: What's your favorite Chinese dish?
